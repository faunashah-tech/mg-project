Our Story

Born Now. Because Health Doesn't Wait.

Bornow Coffee was conceptualised in 2024 by four business management graduates in Surat, Gujarat. We noticed a simultaneous convergence of two massive trends: the undeniable rise of premium coffee culture and the urgent demand for daily wellness.


However, consumers were forced to choose between two separate habits—their delicious morning coffee, and their clinical daily supplements. We decided to eliminate this friction entirely. The name Bornow is a fusion of "Born" and "Now," embodying our ethos that optimal nutrition must be seamlessly woven into the rhythm of daily life.

The Science of MEDS

Micro-Encapsulation Delivery System

The technical challenge at the heart of functional coffee is simple: how do you incorporate heat-sensitive vitamins into a product roasted at over 200°C and brewed with near-boiling water?


Partnering with the Department of Food Technology at a leading Gujarat-based university[cite: 83], we developed our proprietary MEDS framework—a three-stage approach that guarantees bioavailability:

  • Stage 1: Pre-Roast Coating. Heat-stable minerals (like Zinc, Manganese, and Calcium) are infused into the green bean matrix through a controlled aqueous infusion process for 18-24 hours at 12°C before roasting[cite: 86].
  • Stage 2: Post-Roast Micro-Encapsulation. Heat-sensitive vitamins (B-complex, C, D3) are encased in a protective lipid membrane derived from sunflower lecithin[cite: 89]. This "enteric coating" survives brewing and breaks down only in the gut's slightly alkaline environment.
  • Stage 3: Cold-Addition. Probiotics (10 billion CFU) and delicate adaptogenic extracts are vacuum tumbled [cite: 93] post-roast using an OSA-modified starch carrier [cite: 94] to prevent oxidation during grinding and short-term storage.

Sustainability & Impact

Designed for the Planet

Wellness extends beyond the individual to the environment we inhabit. Bornow Coffee is deeply committed to minimizing our environmental footprint without compromising the freshness of our specialty blends.


Sustainable Sourcing: All our coffee is sourced from estates holding or pursuing Rainforest Alliance Certification. Through our Bornow Farmer Connect Programme, we work directly with farming families in Coorg, providing agricultural training and guaranteed purchase contracts.


Eco-Packaging: Our current pouches achieve 72% recycled material content using kraft paper and bio-based laminates. Our Target: By FY28, all Bornow Coffee packaging will be 100% fully compostable or recyclable.
