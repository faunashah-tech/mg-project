The Science: MEDS Technology

Our proprietary Micro-Encapsulation Delivery System (MEDS) solves the ultimate challenge: protecting heat-sensitive vitamins during roasting and brewing[cite: 82, 83].

Using a food-grade lipid micro-encapsulation carrier, we encase vitamins in a protective membrane that survives the brewing process and ensures bioavailability in the gut[cite: 89, 90].
