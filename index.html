Our Story

Born Now. Because Health Doesn't Wait.

Bornow Coffee was conceptualised in 2024 by four business management graduates in Surat, Gujarat. We noticed a simultaneous convergence of two massive trends: the undeniable rise of premium coffee culture and the urgent, post-pandemic demand for daily wellness.


However, consumers were forced to choose between two separate habits—their delicious morning coffee, and their clinical daily supplements. We decided to eliminate this friction entirely. The name Bornow is a fusion of "Born" and "Now," embodying our ethos that optimal nutrition must be seamlessly woven into the rhythm of daily life.

The Science of MEDS

Micro-Encapsulation Delivery System

The technical challenge at the heart of functional coffee is simple: how do you incorporate heat-sensitive vitamins into a product roasted at over 200°C and brewed with boiling water?


Partnering with food technology experts, we developed our proprietary MEDS framework:

  • Stage 1: Pre-Roast Coating. Heat-stable minerals (like Zinc and Calcium) are infused into the green bean matrix before roasting.
  • Stage 2: Post-Roast Micro-Encapsulation. Heat-sensitive vitamins (B-complex, C, D3) are encased in a protective lipid membrane that survives brewing and breaks down only in the gut for maximum bioavailability.
  • Stage 3: Cold-Addition. Probiotics and delicate extracts are vacuum tumbled post-roast to prevent oxidation.

Sustainable Packaging

Designed for the Planet

Wellness extends beyond the individual to the environment we inhabit. Bornow Coffee is deeply committed to minimizing our environmental footprint without compromising the freshness of our specialty blends.


Our Phase 1 packaging utilizes kraft paper stand-up pouches with a bio-based laminate and a recyclable aluminium degassing valve. This currently achieves approximately 72% recycled material content.


Our Target: By FY28, all Bornow Coffee packaging will be 100% fully compostable or recyclable, aligning with our broader goal to achieve carbon neutrality across all operations by 2030.
